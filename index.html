Zainab Husain
I am a journalist in Pakistan with an interest in gender justice, urban planning and equitable cities. I am the managing editor of Soch Fact Check and a video producer at Soch Videos, where I have produced a number of short documentaries about Pakistani politics, gender inequality, city planning and human rights violations.

I have a Bachelor's degree in Social Anthropology from SOAS, University of London. In my free time I like to read fiction, scuba dive and avoid social media.
